Breaking News

We have a new name.

 

Napier Concert Otchestra has become Napier Community Orchestra.

 

The pupose of this is to better refelct what we do and who we are.

 

Who we are is simple. We are a group of people, from tennagers to real decadents ,who get together on a regular basis to make music. Mainly classical , but whatever type of music we undertake we do do with the aim of doing it well and to the best of our abilities.

 

What we do , or at least aim to do, is to serve our community. We are, in all senses of the word, volunteers . We derive great pleasure from what we do musically and our object is to share this with the community at large but particularly our local one.
