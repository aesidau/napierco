2007

October Concert

On 21st October,2007 we took great joy in accompanying Kathryn Taylor, violinist with the Melbourne Symphony Orchestra, in a performance of the Beethoven violin concerto.
The audience were as delighted as were we and many claimed it to be the Napier's best performance yet.
