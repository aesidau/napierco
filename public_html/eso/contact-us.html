Join our mailing list

By subscribing to our mailing list -- it is totally free -- you will get advance notice of concerts and events involving the orchestra.

If you give us an email address these notices will come by email.

If you would like to subscribe a family member who has not got email access then simply let us know their name and address and notices will be dispatched to them through normal post.

All we ask is that in the email you include your name, address, telephone contact ( sometimes times get altered at short notice ).

Just click on the link below to start your email:

Email The Essendon Symphony Mailing List

If you have sent an email to us previously in 2012, please send again to the updated email address in this link. We have had problems with the previous address, and apologise if we have not responded to you.

Thank you from all of us.
