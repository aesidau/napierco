Next concert: Memories and Music from WWI

2pm, Sunday 11th September

The Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds

This free concert marks the conclusion of a series of four concerts at the Clocktower Centre to commemorate the First World War. The performers in this concert have all performed one of the previous concerts in the series, and now come together in a single brilliant concert. We will be joined by the Essendon Choral Society and the Moonee Valley Brass, and the afternoon will feature music from the World War One era, including popular songs and instrumental classics.
