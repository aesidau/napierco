Next concert: Symphonic Bites

Sunday 31st August, 2:30pm

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds VIC 3039

Our last concert was only in July, but our next concert is full of music that you'd want to listen to every month. We have put together a programme packed with all your favourite classical pieces, so it's extremely accessible and lots of fun. If you love finishing a concert with a smile on your face and humming a catchy tune, this is the concert for you!

Pieces include:

  • Beethoven - 5th Symphony (movement 1)
  • Smetana - Dance of the Comedians
  • Mendelssohn - Hebrides Overture (Fingal's Cave)

Duration: approximately 60-70 minutes with no interval.

Buy tickets

Tickets are only $15 for adults and $10 concession, and are available from either the Clocktower box office on 03 9243 9191 or through their website.

STOP PRESS! If you bring a group of four people or more, adult tickets are available at concession prices!
