Next concert: Animal Antics Kids Concert

2:30pm, Sunday 23rd August

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds VIC 3039

This year we see a return of our famous kids concert. We'll be playing all your favourite pieces about animals, and encourage you to attend dressed as an animal also! Pieces include:

  • Saint-Saens - Selections of The Carnival of the Animals
  • Tchaikovsky - Selections from Swan Lake Suite
  • Rimsky-Korsakov - Flight of the Bumblebee
  • Rossini - The Thieving Magpie Overture
Duration: around 1 hr with no interval.

If you are a parent or teacher who would like to print out some material for kids about the concert, we have some handy pages for you to download.

Buy tickets

Adults are at children's prices: all tickets at $15. Tickets can be bought online or by calling the box office on 9243 9191.
