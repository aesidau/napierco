Soloist Sydney Braunfeld

Next concert: Scheherazade

Sunday 16th November, 2:30pm

Melba Hall, Gate 12, Royal Pde, The University of Melbourne, Parkville VIC 3052

Come along and hear some stunning music in one of Melbourne's most loved concert halls. Our upcoming concert is part of our conductor's Master of Music Performers degree, so you get a chance to hear it for free.

Our violin soloist, Rohan Carr, is one of our orchestra's own talents, having lead the orchestra in several concerts.

Our horn soloist, Sydney Braunfeld, is an up and coming French horn player hailing from New York. She holds a bachelor's and a master's degree from The Juilliard School, and has performed with numerous professional American orchestras. Since moving to Australia in 2010, Sydney has established herself in the orchestral scene, performing regularly with the Melbourne Symphony Orchestra and Orchestra Victoria. Last year, she performed a Strauss Horn Concerto with the Melbourne Sinfonia, and has recently completed her first regional tour of the country as principal horn with Opera Australia. Next year, Sydney is looking forward to joining the Hong Kong Philharmonic Orchestra on their tour of Europe.

Pieces performed include:

  • Lilburn - Aotearoa Overture
  • Mozart - Horn Concerto no. 4 (soloist: Sydney Braunfeld)
  • Rimsky-Korsakov - Scheherazade (soloist: Rohan Carr)

Duration: approximately one and half hours.
