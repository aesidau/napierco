Next concert: Mozart, Mendelssohn and More

3pm, Sunday 3rd June

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds

A free community concert with the West Melbourne Schools Orchestra as part of the Moonee Valley Council "Winter Music in the Valley" series.

Our concert will be about 1hr long, and includes well-loved music by Mozart, Mendelssohn and others. The WMSO will be performing by themselves and then with the Essendon Symphony for two combined numbers.

See also the Clocktower Centre event page.
