Next Concert: Beautiful Melodies

2:30pm, Sunday 5th August

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds

This is the second in our very popular series bringing some of the most beautiful and popular pieces of classical music to the Clocktower and the people of Moonee Valley. From the dark and mysterious opening chords of Mozart’s Don Giovanni Overture to the moving and much-loved Beethoven’s 7th, it promises to be a wonderful afternoon of music.

We are delighted to welcome Arna Morton as our soloist who is currently a Performance PhD candidate, Academic Assistant, and recipient of the Australian Postgraduate Award at the University of Melbourne Conservatorium of Music.

  • Don Giovanni Overture - Mozart
  • Violin Concerto No. 1 - Prokofiev (featuring soloist Arna Morton)
  • Symphony No. 7 - Beethoven

Duration: approximately 100 minutes, including a brief interval.

See also the Clocktower Centre event page.
