Next concert: Stage Door

3:00pm, Saturday 25th November

Essendon Baptist Church, 134-138 Buckley St, Essendon (opposite St Columba's College)

Our final concert for the year will be a more relaxed affair! We will be performing a range of music from the stage, most of which you are sure to be familiar with. Pieces include:

  • American in Paris - Gershwin
  • HMS Pinafore Overture - Gilber and Sullivan
  • Polonaise from Eugene Onegin - Tchaikovsky
  • In the Steppes of Central Asia - Borodin
  • Fidelio Overture - Beethoven
  • Horn Concerto No. 1 (1st Movement) - Strauss (featuring Kristie Baker)

We will also be joined by special guests, the West Melbourne Schools Orchestra.

Buy tickets

Tickets are available in advance online for just $12 from TryBooking, or can be purchased at the venue on the day for $20 adults / $12 children and concession. Seating is General Admission.
