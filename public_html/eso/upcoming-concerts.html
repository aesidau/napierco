Next concert: Tubby the Tuba and Other Super Heroes

2:30pm, Sunday 6th May

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds

Do you know the story of Tubby the Tuba from your own childhood? A quick refresher: Tubby the Tuba is sad because he never gets to play the melody. He goes in search of his own melody, and leads us through an exciting musical tale.

Our children's concert features our musical telling of Tubbyâ€™s story, with a Narrator to guide us and, of course, a tuba soloist! This is an educational and fun performance that features all sections of the orchestra - including the highly underrated tuba!

The concert will also feature a range of other pieces from the wonderful world of classical and popular music, including

  • Superman
  • Battle of the Heroes (from Star Wars)
  • The Torreadors from Carmen

Kids are encouraged to dress up as their favourite superhero.

This one hour concert, with audience interaction, is sure to be lots of fun for kids of all ages!
