Next concert: Christmas Celebration

Sunday 15th December, 2.30pm

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds VIC 3039

The Essendon Symphony brings a Christmas Celebration to the Clocktower! Immerse yourself in the great European Christmas tradition, like the Vienna New Years Concert and London's Proms. Waltz, sing and clap along to family favourites. Enjoy the jazz stylings of Michelle Nicolle, vocal splendour from the Essendon Chorale and the fun and love of music that is your Christmas present from the ESO.

Pieces include:

  • Strauss - Emperor Waltzes
  • Walton - Crown Imperial Coronation March
  • Tschaikovsky - Three Dances from The Nutcracker
  • Strauss - Radetzky March
  • Handel - Hallelujah Chorus
  • and don't forget - Sing-a-long Carols!

Duration: Approx two hours including interval.

Also: Carols by Candlelight

Saturday 14th December, 7:30pm

Napier Park (near corner of Noble Avenue and Bruce St), Strathmore VIC 3041

These much-loved public carols are well-attended by both nearby residents and those who travel across Melbourne to experience this unique local tradition that first started in 1948. Bring a picnic and a rug, and settle on the grass with your family, waving candles (fire restrictions permitting) or torches in time with the music. All are welcome. Audience participation is expected!

Please note that Noble Avenue will be blocked for traffic, if you are coming to the Carols by car.
