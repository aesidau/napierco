Next concert: Last Night of the Proms

2:30pm, Sunday 21st August

The Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds

After many requests, we are doing it again, and putting on our own version of the Last Night of the Proms. We are joined by the Moonee Valley Community Choir and The Essendon Choral Society to give you an afternoon you won't forget. Come dressed in your British-themed clothes, or even just as you are, and have fun as we mix serious with silly.

Pieces include:

  • Tchaikovsky - Symphony No. 5 First Movement
  • Sibelius - Finlandia
  • Grainger - Shepherd's Hey
  • Mangione - Children of Sanchez
  • Arne - Rule, Brittania!
  • Elgar - Pomp and Circumstance

Duration: approx. 1 hour and 45 minutes, including interval.

Buy tickets

Adults are $30, and children and concession tickets are $20.

Book tickets by calling the box office on 9243 9191 or on the Clocktower Centre website.
