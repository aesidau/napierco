Next concert: Moonee Valley Winter Series

Sunday 6th July, 3pm

Clocktower Centre, 750 Mt Alexander Rd, Moonee Ponds VIC 3039

The Essendon Symphony will bring the Moonee Valley Winter Series of concerts at the Clocktower Centre to a close, with a memorable concert featuring the stunning Bruch Violin Concerto. We are thrilled to have Cameron Jamieson join us as the violin soloist for that piece.

Pieces include:

  • Bruch - Violin Concerto
  • Borodin - Prince Igor Overture
  • Smetana - Dance of the Comedians

Coming up: Symphonic Bites

Sunday 31st August, 2:30pm

Clocktower Centre

Save the date for this fun concert that is full of familiar pieces. If you love finishing a concert with a smile on your face and humming a catchy tune, this is the concert for you!

Pieces include:

  • Beethoven - 5th Symphony (movement 1)
  • Mendelssohn - Hebrides Overture (Fingal's Cave)
