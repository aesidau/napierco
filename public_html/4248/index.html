About Us

The orchestra was founded in the 1990's by Paul Green (who still plays with us ) as a string ensemble and at one stage comprised no one but strings. It has over the years waxed and waned in numbers but there are always positions available for musicians who play the violin, viola, cello, or double bass. Additionally we need permanent brass (Trumpet, French horn), and oboe.

 

The Napier Community Orchestra is still, as far as we know, the only community orchestra in Melbourne’s western suburbs.
