Membership

Newcomers are welcome to play with us for two rehearsals to get a feel for the orchestra before committing to membership. Players need to have the equivalent of Grade 5-7 AMEB.particularly as regards sight reading ability.

We are a fee paying orchestra and fees are payable at the beginning of each school term. We are also always on the lookout for community, municipal, government and philanthropic grants. Membership Fees are charged to cover the costs of music hire and purchase, venue hire, conductor salaries, advertising, stationary, concert production and public liability insurance.

  • $30 per term

Joining the Orchestra

If and when you come to join, please be sure to hand to Ruth Mackinnon or Rosey (our treasurer ) a slip of paper containing your name, contact telephone numbers - home and mobile if you have it - and your email address for future contact.
